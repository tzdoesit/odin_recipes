Homestyle Garlic Bread

Extra Garlic!

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Parsley (Optional)

Instructions:

  1. Start toasting your bread
  2. Crush your garlic
  3. Mix your garlic with your butter
  4. Heat butter and garlic mixture in microwave or stove until softened
  5. Laver the heated mixture onto your bread
  6. Enjoy!
