Kani Salad!

Healthy. Fresh. Delicious.

Ingredients

  • Kani (Imitation Crab meat)
  • Cucumber
  • Sriracha
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Sesame Seeds (Optional)
  • Carrots (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Take your cucumber and cut into small strips, about 2 inches long.
  2. Peel or slice your kani into strips
  3. Take your carrots if you decide to add carrots and make them into strips too
  4. Combine all ingredients into a bowl
  5. Add sriracha and mayo to taste
  6. Mix it well
  7. Enjoy!
