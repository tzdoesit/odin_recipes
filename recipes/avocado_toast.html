How to make Avocado Toast

The Easy Way!

Ingredients:

  • Avocados (Ripe)
  • Sourdough or French Bread
  • Lemon
  • Yogurt (Optional)
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions:

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Peel and transfer avocado to a medium-large bowl
  3. Take a fork and start mashing the avocado, but make sure not to mush them too much. Leave a little bit of chunks left.
  4. Add a small squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt
  5. Lavish on avocado onto your freshly toasted bread
  6. Sprinkle finishing salt and top with red chili flakes if you like
  7. You're all done!
